Plum Radio

a tool to help writers get tuned in

random poem fragments provide a starting point for freewriting. gut mode is timed: 60 seconds to get words flowing. zen mode is untimed, take as long as you want. you can change mode any time in the top bar. click the "past lines" tab to see what you've written for each fragment.
